Welcome to UShould

We have a sensor that a user can stick on anything and an Android and iOS application. When the sensor moves, the mobile app timer is reset (Congrats, you remembered to do something and are on your way to forming a habit!). If the sensor doesn't move after a certain period of time and the timers hit zero, the iOS and Android Apps send push notifications to the user telling them to do the required activity (and thus forming a habit).
